Geographical Location of Japan

Japan is located in Northeast Asia, bordered to the east by the Pacific Ocean, and surrounded to the west by the Sea of Japan, the Korea Strait, the East China Sea, Siberia on the Eurasian continent, the Korean Peninsula, and mainland China. To the south, it is adjacent to the Philippine Sea, Taiwan, and the Mariana Islands, while to the north, it is bordered by the Soya Strait, the Sea of Okhotsk, and the Kuril Islands. Japan experiences a pleasant climate throughout the year, with distinct seasons. In the south, the weather is mild, while it becomes progressively colder towards the north. Particularly in winter, the northwest monsoon brings heavy snowfall on the Sea of Japan side. On the other hand, the Pacific side continues to experience dry and clear weather known as 'winter clear.
